# NOOP v8.3.4

A focused fix for a confusing sync message. Update from the Releases page, AltStore, or `brew upgrade`.

## Fixed

- **No more false "charge to 100%" alarm right after a successful sync (#42).** When NOOP auto-continues an offload to drain the last of your strap's banked history, the final (empty) pass could print the alarming "strap has no banked history — fully charge it and reconnect" line and count it as an empty sync — even though the same sync had just banked hundreds of records. NOOP now recognises that tail as "caught up" and keeps the real charge-to-100% guidance for straps that genuinely aren't banking. Both platforms.
